## 💪 What is GRITZONE?

GRITZONE is a mobile-first fitness platform built for people who take their grind seriously. Track workouts, scan meals with AI, earn badges, and hold your squad accountable — all in one dark, clean interface designed for daily use.

---

## 🔥 Features

| | Feature | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 🏋️ | **Hevy-style Workout Logger** | Live timer, 70+ exercises by muscle group, set tracking (kg × reps), workout photos |
| 📷 | **AI Food Scanner** | Snap a photo of your plate → AI identifies every item with macros. That simple. |
| 🍽️ | **Healthify-style Meal Tracker** | 90+ Indian foods with serving sizes pre-filled. Search "upma" → see 1 cup, 170 cal, 4g protein. One tap to log. |
| 🏆 | **50+ Badges & Achievements** | From "Day One" to "Year of GRIT". Rare, Epic, Legendary tiers. Flex them on your profile. |
| 👥 | **Social Feed** | See your squad's workouts, meals, and check-ins. Configurable — show what you want. |
| 🤝 | **Referral System** | Invite friends → both get +30 days beta credit. Stack rewards. |
| 📊 | **Daily Check-ins** | Weight, steps, water, sleep, workout — one form, every day, no excuses. |
| 🧪 | **Beta Rewards** | Every day you use GRITZONE in beta = 1 free day when Pro launches. |
